YOUR PRIVATE PARADISE

Koulen Central

Siem Reap

Located in Siem Reap, 800 metres from King's Road Angkor, Koulen Central Hotel provides accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden and a terrace. 6 km from Angkor Wat and 700 metres from Preah Ang Chek Preah Ang Chom, the property offers a restaurant and a bar. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

The Sanctuary Villa

Battambang

Designed to look like a Khmer Buddhist Temple, The Sanctuary Villa Battambang offers an outdoor pool. Fitted with free WiFi, air-conditioned villas present a mix of modern and traditional decor. The Sanctuary Villa Battambang offers a complimentary pickup service from Battambang Bus Terminal or train station. Via tuk-tuk, it is a 5-minute ride from the town area and a 15-minute ride from Bamboo Train Station.

Montagne Boutique

kampot

Located in Kampot and with Kampot Pagoda reachable within 4.7 km, Montagne Boutique provides concierge services, non-smoking rooms, a garden, free WiFi and a restaurant. Each accommodation at the 3-star hotel has city views, and guests can enjoy access to an indoor pool. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, a tour desk and currency exchange for guests.
